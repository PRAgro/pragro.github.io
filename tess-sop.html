Solucros® Sulphate of Potash

Solucros® has all the necessary characteristics to be ideal for nutrigation. It is a pure, highly concentrated, fully soluble fertilizer that is easy to handle. When used for nutrigation, we recommend that Solucros® is dissolved first, before adding any other fertilizers. With very alkaline water (pH > 8), first acidify the solution before adding the Solucros®When used for foliar nutrition, Solucros® has proved to be highly efficient in curing or preventing potassium deficiency as well as improving the quality of fruits and vegetables. Foliar sprays containing Solucros® should be applied during the evening or early morning hours while the crops are in a turgid state.SOP (sulphate of potash), provides one of the mildest forms of the important plant nutrient, potassium. The fertilizer offers a number of important benefits over other sources of potassium, which enable to help produce high quality crops with maximum export value, as well as to protect the environment.Solucros® contains a minimum of 50.5% K20 and 18% Sulphur. Intensive cropping combined with limited sulphur replacement has created an increasing sulphur deficiency in some soils. Solucros® ensures optimum uptake of both potassium and sulphur (as sulphate)
