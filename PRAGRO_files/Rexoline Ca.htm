REXOLIN® Ca

Main Characteristics : Rexolin® Ca is a stable, water-soluble and non-dusting calcium chelate; Calcium is chelated by EDTA.

Application : For foliar, side and top dressing, and for Hydroponics
Appearance :  Free flowing white micro-granules
pH (1% solution) : 6.5 - 7.5
Bulk density untapped : approx. 650 - 850 kg/m3
Solubility in water  : approx. 800 g/l (20 ºC), >1,500 g/l (80 ºC)
