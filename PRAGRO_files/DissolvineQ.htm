Chemical Name :  Ethylenediamine-N,N'-bis(2-hydroxy-phenylacetic acid) ferric-sodium complex; EDDHA-FeNa
Dissolvine® Q  is a product developed for usage on iron deficient soils with wide range of pH ranging from 3-10. It has an excellent performance in alkaline high pH Soil  where EDTA will not work.
When the pH near the roots is rising to 7.5 or higher, or when the water used contains very high levels of zinc, it is advised to use Dissolvine® Q
