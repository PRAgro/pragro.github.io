CaNsol® Calcium Nitrate

CaNsol® is Solution Grade calcium nitrate that help ensure high value, highly marketable produce.  Quite simply, it is a high quality fertilizer that optimizes cash-crop performance and produce food that satisfies even the most demanding growers and consumers.

 CaNsol® provides fast-acting nitrate-N, alongside strength-building calcium.  In combination, these nutrients fuel prolonged growth.  At the same time, plants and trees treated with CaNsol® branded fertilizer are naturally healthier and less sensitive to stress during growth.  Then, critically, in the build-up to harvest, CaNsol® improves the size, strength and appearance of the fruit, tuber, flower or leaf. 

The end result is blemish free produce that will store longer and which is less susceptible to damage, disease or rotting and visibly more desirable, colorful, fresher, and highly nutritious.

 Advantages of CaNsol®

  • Efficiently absorbed by plants.

  • Consists of 100% plant nutrients.
  • High source of calcium for plants (26.3% CaO / 18.8% Ca).
  • Free of chloride, sodium and other detrimental elements for plants.
  • Suitable for nutrigation, foliar nutrition.

Double benefit: CaNsol® is not only an efficient source of calcium, but also facilitates plant uptake  the K & Mg present in the soil. This is due to the nitrate nitrogen (NO3- ) in CaNsol®, which helps uptake of positively charged ions (cations) like K+, Ca++ & Mg+ etc.
