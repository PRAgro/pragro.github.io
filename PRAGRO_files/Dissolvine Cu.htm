Dissolvine® Cu

Main Characteristics : Dissolvine® Cu is a stable, water-soluble and non-dusting copper chelate; Copper is chelated by EDTA.

Application : For foliar, side and top dressing, and for Hydroponics
Appearance : Blue micro-granules
pH (1% solution) : 6 - 7
Bulk density untapped : approx. 600 - 800 kg/m3
Solubility in water  : approx. 1,200 g/l (20 ºC), 1,700 g/l (80 ºC)
