AgriPLUS™ Mono-Potassium Phosphate 0-52-34 

AgriPLUS™ MKP is a fully water-soluble mono-potassium phosphate fertilizer, a highly efficient source of  phosphorus and potassium for plants. As a nitrogen-free fertilizer, AgriPLUS™MKP is the preferred source of phosphorus and potassium when nitrogen fertilization should be limited. A common case is at early growing season, when phosphorus and potassium are needed at high rates for the establishment of root system. Application of AgriPLUS™MKP at the productive stages of sugar-rich fruit crops helps to increase sugar content and to improve the quality of these.

AgriPLUS™ MKP can be applied in combination with other fertilizers to meet crop nutritional needs throughout the growth cycle. It's high purity and water-solubility make AgriPLUS™ MKP an ideal fertilizer for nutrigation and for foliar nutrition.

Additionally, AgriPLUS™MKP is suitable for preparation of fertilizer blends and production of liquid fertilizers. When applied as foliar spray, AgriPLUS™ MKP acts as a suppressor of powdery mildew.

Advantages of AgriPLUS™ MKP

  • Fully Water Soluble.
  • Consists of 100% plant nutrients.
  • Free of chloride, sodium and other detrimental elements.
  • Safe for plants, has a moderately low pH and low salt index .
